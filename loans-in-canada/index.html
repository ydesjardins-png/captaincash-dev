Who qualifies

Who Captain Cash loans are for

Our income-based model opens access to Canadians who would typically be turned down by traditional lenders.

Employed Canadians in a cash crunch

You have a steady job and pay your bills on time, but an unexpected expense — car repair, medical bill, utility payment — hit before payday. A $500–$1,000 installment loan bridges the gap without disrupting your cash flow.

Canadians with bad or no credit

Past financial difficulty, a thin credit file, or no credit history — none of these disqualify you. We look at your current income and banking stability, not a credit score from years ago.

New Canadians building credit

If you recently arrived in Canada, you may have a thin or non-existent credit file — even if you have an excellent financial history elsewhere. We assess what you have now, not what a credit bureau says.

Borrowers who need money today

Bank applications take days. Captain Cash applications take 5 minutes and funds arrive the same business day. If your need is urgent, our process is built around your timeline — not ours.
