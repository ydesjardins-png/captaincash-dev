To qualify, you must
  • Be a Canadian resident, 18+, in a served province
  • Earn at least $1,200/month by direct deposit

  • Have few or no NSF transactions

We do not accept these income sources
  • Social assistance & welfare
  • Student loans
  • Child tax benefit
  • CPP / Private pension
  • Disability income
  • Other insurance income
