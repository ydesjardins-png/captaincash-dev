Eligibility

Do you qualify?

Most Canadians do. Here is what you need to apply:

  • Canadian resident, 18 years or older
  • Monthly income of at least $1,200
  • Employed for at least 3 months
  • Active Canadian bank account (3+ months old)
  • Not currently bankrupt or in a consumer proposal

Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Will you check my credit score?

No. We do not perform a traditional credit check. We review your income and banking history to assess eligibility instead.

How fast will I get my money?

If you are approved before 2 PM ET on a business day, funds are sent by Interac e-Transfer the same day.

What if I need to defer a payment?

Contact us at least 48 hours before your payment date. A deferral fee of $25 applies. We work with you — not against you.

Can I repay early?

Yes. You can repay your loan early at any time with no penalty whatsoever.
