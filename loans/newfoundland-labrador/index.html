Who qualifies

Eligibility for a Captain Cash loan in Newfoundland and Labrador

Our income-based model is open to Newfoundland and Labrador residents who meet these minimum requirements.

You must:

✓Canadian resident, 18 years or older
✓Currently residing in Newfoundland and Labrador
✓Earn at least $1,200/month by direct deposit
✓3+ months stable employment or consistent income
✓Active Canadian bank account open for 3+ months
