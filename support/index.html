Eligibility & Applying

Yes. Captain Cash does not perform a traditional credit check. We assess your application based on your income and banking history — not your credit score. Applying will not place a hard inquiry on your credit file and will not affect your score.

You must be a Canadian resident aged 18+, earn at least $1,200/month by direct deposit, have 3+ months of stable employment, hold an active Canadian bank account (3+ months), have few or no NSF transactions, not be bankrupt or in a consumer proposal, and reside in a served province.

The following are not accepted for new applications: social assistance, student loans, child tax benefit, CPP, private pensions, disability income, and other insurance income. Employment Insurance (EI) may be accepted for renewals after a successful first repayment.

We are licensed in Ontario, British Columbia, Alberta, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, PEI, Newfoundland & Labrador, Nunavut, and the Northwest Territories. We are not currently available in Quebec, Manitoba, or Saskatchewan.

Our application is 100% online — no documents to scan, fax, or mail. We use instant bank verification (read-only access to your statement) and an e-signature platform. The entire process is completed from any device in minutes.

Yes. Your application must include at least one valid phone number registered in your name. This is used to verify your application and communicate your decision.

Costs & Repayment

Our APR is 23%. A $500 loan repays approximately $750 total over 90–150 days. A $1,000 loan repays approximately $1,500. The full repayment schedule and total cost are shown in your agreement before you sign — no surprises.

NSF fee: $45 if a payment fails. Deferral fee: $25 with 48 hours notice. Application fee: free. Early repayment penalty: none. All fees are disclosed in your loan agreement before you sign.

Repayments are collected automatically from the bank account you provided, on dates that align with your pay schedule — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. The full schedule is shown before you sign.

Security & Privacy

Absolutely not. We never see your username or password, and we have no ability to log in to your account. The bank verification system provides read-only access to your statement only. Your credentials are never stored or accessible by our team.

Yes. All data is protected by SSL encryption. We are a direct lender — not a broker — so your information is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers or lead generators.

We use industry-standard instant bank verification (IBV) — a secure, read-only view of your recent bank statement to verify income and account activity. It cannot move, withdraw, or access your funds in any way.

Your loan agreement is signed via a secure e-signature platform. E-signatures are legally binding in Canada under the Electronic Commerce Act and equivalent provincial legislation — no printing or mailing required.

Captain Cash is a direct lender. We review, approve, and fund all loans in-house. Your application and personal information are never sold or shared with third-party lenders, brokers, or advertisers. You deal with our team from start to finish.
