After you receive your funds

What happens once you have your money

Understanding repayment, deferral, and renewal before you apply helps you borrow with confidence.

Repayment schedule

Your repayments are automatically withdrawn from the bank account you provided, on dates that align with your pay schedule. You choose weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly when you accept your loan. Each payment amount is fixed and shown in your agreement before you sign.

Need to defer a payment?

If you need to postpone a payment, contact our team at least 48 hours before your scheduled date. A deferral fee of $25 applies. We work with you — not against you. You can reach us at 1-888-226-1026 on any business day.

Early repayment

You can repay your loan in full at any time with no prepayment penalty. Paying early reduces the total amount you owe, since interest accrues daily on the outstanding balance. Contact our team to arrange early repayment.

Loan renewal

Once your loan is fully repaid, customers in good standing may qualify to renew. Returning customers typically benefit from a faster review process. Visit the renew page or call 1-888-226-1026 to find out if you are eligible.

What if a payment fails?

If a scheduled payment cannot be processed due to insufficient funds, a non-sufficient funds (NSF) fee of $45 is charged. To avoid this, contact us before your payment date if you anticipate an issue. We encourage proactive communication.

Always here to help

Our Halifax-based team is available Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM ET. Call 1-888-226-1026, or use the contact form on our support page. We respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible.

Honest advice

Is a short-term loan right for you?

We want to help — but we also want to be honest. A short-term loan is not the right solution for every situation.

Captain Cash may be a good fit if you

  • Have an urgent, one-time expense (car repair, utility bill, medical cost)
  • Have a stable income but need cash before your next paycheque
  • Have been declined by a bank due to credit history
  • Need funds faster than a traditional bank can provide
  • Can comfortably manage the repayment installments within your budget

Consider other options if you

  • Are already struggling to repay existing debts
  • Need the money for regular ongoing expenses (not a one-time need)
  • Are not confident you can meet the repayment schedule
  • Have time to wait — a traditional bank loan at lower rates may be more appropriate
  • Are considering bankruptcy or in a consumer proposal

If you are facing serious debt issues, free credit counselling is available across Canada. Contact the Credit Counselling Society at 1-888-527-8999.

Borrow responsibly

Short-term loans are not a long-term solution

Captain Cash is here when you need emergency funds — not as a substitute for financial planning. We genuinely encourage our customers to view short-term borrowing as a temporary bridge, not a recurring solution.

If you find yourself borrowing repeatedly to cover everyday expenses, it may be a signal that a budget review or credit counselling would be more beneficial than another loan. A regular savings plan — even a small one — can reduce your need to borrow in the future.

For serious debt concerns, free and confidential help is available across Canada from non-profit credit counselling organizations.
